# ğŸ§© Structure of the Policy

The Incident Response Policy is organized into the following sections:

Section	Description

- Statement of Management Commitment	Formal acknowledgment of executive support for incident response readiness and resources.

- Purpose of the Policy	Defines the intent and objectives guiding incident response within the organization.

- Objectives of the Policy	Outlines measurable goals such as minimizing downtime, preserving evidence, and improving detection capabilities.

- Scope of the Policy	Describes the systems, users, and data covered by the policy.

- Definition of Information Security Incidents	Provides clear definitions and examples of what constitutes an incident.

- Organizational Structure and Roles	Defines the Incident Response Team (IRT), including analysts, coordinators, and executives.

- Levels of Authority	Clarifies escalation thresholds and decision-making responsibilities.

- Reporting Requirements	Details internal escalation timelines and external regulatory or partner communication procedures.
